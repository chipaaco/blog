I'm a poor argentinean system's student, I hardly can afford a 20 usd/month suscription for AI and It really hurts.

Recently I've been thinking about how can I spend less money and keep ahead of the curve in this AI consoomer world. I've tried google's plans, and I've to say I love notebooklm, but considering the short usage quotas antigravity and gemini-cli offers, I cannot justify it as my main ai toolchain... I've to mention that having 5TB of g-drive is insanely good, but my needs are really good-covered with just the 200gb of the plus plan.

I think Gemini models are really good for thinking and orchestrating tasks, of course Claude Opus ones are the bests, but also the most expensives.

Considering I use Google Drive, Notebooklm, and Gemini app a lot, I shold maintain the 5 usd plus suscription.

But what about coding agents? I've been researching a lot, I haven't found a most cost-efficient western company suscription better than Github Copilot. With 10$ / month you get a generous ammont of quota, and a variety of models for choose, even with the recent enshitification. They also offers to verified student's an incredible 2 years free usage plan.

Before the shinking of the AI bubble, the student's plan also offered access to Claude Opus 4.6, GPT 5.4 for choosing 🙀, was a good time, Even that passed away, having the oportunity to use infinite gpt-5.0-mini for free for agenti tasks is awesome, the most advanced model i have with this student plan is gpt-5.3 codex, it's really good but counts as x1 premium request. I'll clarify the quotas system in the future.

Also I'll expand one of the most underated afirmation "copilot-cli is the most performant, beutiful and well-tested looking agent cli program i've used". I tested gemini-cli and opencode, and copilot one is the winner. I love the mascot, i love the startup time, I love the feeling of usage. It's mostly perfect.
