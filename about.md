Hi, I'm Chipaaco.

This blog is made for me first.

I'm a native Spanish speaker, so the writing here may be in Spanish, English, or a mix of both.

Some posts are carefully edited, and others may include LLM-generated text when I have less time.

I'm not trying to chase fame with this project. I just want to experiment, shape something I enjoy, and sometimes share opinions or ideas I value with people I trust or with strangers.

In other words, this is my small take on the classic web.
